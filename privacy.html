Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Comsol ("Comsol", "we", "us") handles information when you visit comsol.digital or contact us about our Windows support subscription or license trading services. Comsol is an independent IT service provider and provides all support and enquiries by email.

01Information we collect

We keep data collection to the minimum needed to respond to you:

  • Information you send us by email. When you use the contact form on our site, your browser composes an email in your own mail application — nothing is transmitted to or stored by this website. When you send that email, we receive the details you chose to include (such as your name, company, work email, the topic, approximate seat or license quantity, and your message).
  • Correspondence. The content of emails you exchange with us, which we retain to provide and document our services.
  • Basic technical data. Standard server and analytics information such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited, where applicable, used to keep the site secure and understand aggregate usage.

We do not collect a phone number, and the website does not include payment processing or account creation.

02How we use information

  • To respond to your enquiry and provide the subscription or license service you requested.
  • To prepare quotes, invoices, and service records, which we issue by email.
  • To maintain the security, integrity, and performance of our website.
  • To comply with our legal and regulatory obligations.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for unrelated advertising profiling.

03Legal basis & advertising

We process the information you provide to take steps at your request prior to entering a contract, and to pursue our legitimate interest in operating a B2B service. If you reached us through an online advertisement, the advertising platform (for example, Google) may set its own cookies subject to its policies; please refer to that provider's privacy notice for details of any measurement or remarketing it performs.

04Sharing

We share information only as needed to operate, and never for sale:

  • With service providers who host our website or email under confidentiality obligations.
  • Where required by law, regulation, or valid legal process.
  • In connection with a business transfer, subject to this policy.

05Retention

We keep enquiry and service correspondence for as long as needed to provide our services and to meet legal, tax, and accounting requirements, after which it is deleted or anonymised.

06Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal information, and to object to certain processing. To exercise any of these, email us at hello@inkassist.net and we will respond within a reasonable, legally required timeframe.

07Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect the information we hold. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to safeguard your data and to limit access to it.

08Changes & contact

We may update this policy from time to time; the "last updated" date above reflects the current version. Questions about this policy or your data can be sent to hello@inkassist.net.
